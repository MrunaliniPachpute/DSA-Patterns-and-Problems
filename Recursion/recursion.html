WONDERING, HOW DOES IT COVER PATTERNS?
These problems cover the most important recursion and backtracking patterns for interviews:

Factorial → Basic recursion with base case and simple recursive call.
Fibonacci Sequence → Simple recursion, optimized with memoization / DP.
Power Set / Subsets → Backtracking, decision tree recursion pattern.

N-Queens Problem → Constraint-based backtracking recursion pattern.

Together, they ensure you understand base recursion, recursive sequence generation, subset generation, permutation generation, and backtracking with constraints in recursion problems.
